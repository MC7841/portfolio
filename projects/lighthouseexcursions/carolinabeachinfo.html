Carolina Beach Info


Geography

Carolina Beach is a small beach town located on an island bordered by the Atlantic Ocean and the Cape Fear River. It's known in the area to be a laid-back small town, a fantastic destination for a family getaway.

Interesting Attractions

State Park: Located along the Cape Fear River, this park has a unique type of wetland that allows various types of carnivorous plants to grow in the area. If spending time outdoors is your style, take some time to check out the Venus Flytrap trail. The park also has a visitor center that displays natural history exhibits.

Boardwalk: Be sure to check out the Boardwalk District of Carolina Beach. It's a vintage boardwalk that's been there since 1887. Since then, it has been reworked to make the area more accessible to the beach and reintroduced amusement rides. Now it's lined with locally owned food shops and includes an ocean-front walkway for easy beach access. This place is popular for hosting various year-round community events such as, weekly music festivals and fireworks.
