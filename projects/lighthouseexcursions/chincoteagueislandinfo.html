Chincoteague Island Info


Geography

Chincoteague is an island peninsula surrounded by the Chesapeake Bay and the Atlantic Ocean. This island is a sandy tract covered with pine forests. The Chesapeake Bay bridge connects the island to the mainland.

Interesting Attractions

Pony Swim: The Island holds this annual tradition that has been held since 1935. The purpose of the pony swim is to auction off the foals to keep the herd on Assateague Island from overpopulating. Any remaining ponies are herded back to the shore and will swim back to Assateague. Be sure to find a good spot to catch the swim!
